For over eighty years young women made their religious vows in this sacred space. The bride's entry down the centre carpet is a moment to treasure. There is a particular intimacy in marrying in this beautiful Chapel as all have an uninterrupted view of the ceremony.

After the ceremony there are endless opportunities for bridal and guest photos. Garden, cloisters, Chapel steps, fernery, the many nooks and pathways will provide you with very special memories. In case of rain the verandahs and cloisters which surround the Convent provide complete shelter.

As this is a Catholic chapel one of the couple should be Catholic and the ceremony must be conducted by a Catholic priest or deacon. Marriage Celebrant of other faiths are welcome to participate.

Our Wedding Coordinator will ensure that you have all the help you need to prepare to marry in this special place.
