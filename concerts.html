Concerts

What's on at the Convent

 

A wonderful series of concerts has been organised for the annual Sacred Spaces Fine Music Concert Series, held at the stunning Sisters of Mercy Convent in Singleton. Put these dates in your diary!

Sacred Spaces Fine Music Concert Series 2013

Sunday 21 April – Duo Guivio
Giuseppe Zangari (Guitar) and Victoria Jacono-Gilmovich (Violin)
Sunday 21 July at 2pm – 'A-cappella Heaven'
Sydney's premier female a-cappella ensemble, the Endeavour Harmony Chorus
Sunday 29 September at 2pm – 'From Russia With Song'
Lilija Sile (soprano), Robert Harris (viola) and Irina Klamka (piano)

Sunday 21 April AT 2PM

Duo
Guivio

 

Giuseppe Zangari (Guitar) and Victoria
Jacono-Gilmovich (Violin)

An afternoon of delightful and eclectic works written and arranged for violin and guitar. It will feature a fusion of French, Italian and Latin classics, together with newly composed Australian compositions.

Info about the performers:
Giuseppe: www.giuseppezangari.com
Victoria: www.victoriaviolin.com.au

ProgramEnquire now

Sunday 21 July at 2pm

A-cappella
Heaven

 

Sydney's premier female a-cappella
ensemble, the Endeavour Harmony Chorus

will present a concert in the Chapel called 'A-cappella Heaven'. Their repertoire includes modern ballads, old jazz standards, musical hits, 60's favourites, gospel, traditional, swing, blues and comedy. The women who make up this dynamic chorus come from all walks of life and all age groups and share a passion for singing and performing.

This concert will be one of the feature events of the Singleton Arts Festival.

Info about the performers: www.endeavourharmony.com

Enquire now

From Russia
With Song

 

Lilija Sile (soprano), Robert Harris (viola) and Irina Klamka (piano)

Incorporating an afternoon of beautiful song and instrumental pieces from Russia.

Concert Details

 

The information provided here applies to all concerts held at the Sisters of Mercy Convent.

Location
Sacred Spaces - The Convent of Mercy,
30 Queen Street, Singleton NSW Australia, in the heart of the Hunter Valley

Tickets
for each concert include afternoon tea and are: $25 adults, $20 concession, $5 school students. Reservations are recommended to guarantee seating. Limited tickets are also available at the door from 1.15pm on the day of each concert.

Reserved seating & Information
Sacred Spaces Office (open Monday to Thursdy mornings) Phone 6572 2398 or email sacredspaces@singmercy.org.au

If you would like to be on the Sacred Spaces Concerts mailing list, please send a message to sacredspaces@singmercy.org.au or phone the Sacred Spaces Office.

We would also love to hear from you if you are interested in helping out at any of our concerts – eg setting up, selling tickets, ushering, baking or serving afternoon tea, packing away etc.
